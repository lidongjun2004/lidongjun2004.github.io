## 答题技巧

### Structure

#### Part 1 (At least 2 sentences)

- **Point** — 直接回答问题
- **Reason** — 给出原因
- **Example** — 举例说明
- **Conclusion** — 简要总结

#### Part 2

- Well utilize the time for preparation
- **Introduction** — 引入话题
- **Main Points** — First, Second, Third, ...
- **Conclusion** — Future / Feelings

---

### Complexity

#### Paraphrase

| Original | Alternatives |
|----------|-------------|
| Yes | absolutely, of course |
| because | since, cause, as, for the reason that, one of the reasons is, the reason why |
| and | in addition, additionally, plus, besides, moreover, furthermore, also, what's more |

#### Long Sentence

尽量使用从句、并列句等复合结构来展示语法能力。

#### Advanced Word

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|-------|-------------|
| very | extremely, quite, so, too |

---

### Fluency

- **Self Correction** — 使用过渡短语自然修正：sorry, I mean, well, ...
- **No Self Repetition** — 避免重复相同的观点
- **Continuity** — 保持语流连贯

---

### Grammar

注意时态一致性、主谓一致、冠词使用。

---

### Intonation

通过语调变化体现情感和强调重点。

---

### Perspective

- **General** — generally, in general, for most people, for the majority, from most people's perspective, people think that, people believe, we believe
- **Personal** — 用自身经历和观点回答

#### Teachers

> Paraphrase: favorite → like / admire / respect

**1. Do you want to be a teacher in the future?**

No, I don't want to be a teacher. I would rather choose to be an AI engineer or programmer.
Because I can easily gain the knowledge of the textbook but I can't explain it clearly to others.

**2. Do you have a favourite teacher?**

Yes, I do have a favorite teacher, who teaches Chemistry and makes the subject plain and easy to learn.
She always tell me some vivid examples regarding the experiment, which is easy to memorize.
It brings out the best in me, so I'm grateful for all the effort she paid for me.

**3. In what way has your favorite teacher helped you?**

When I got a awful grade in Chemistry test, she always took me to her office and support me.
When I calmed down, she analysed the test paper with me and let me know in which aspect could I improve more, which gave me great assist.

**4. Do you have a teacher from your past that you still remember?**

Yes, I do have a teacher I still remember, who is my math teacher in my junior high school.
I can still recall her because she thinks I'm a very smart student and can have great success in the future, whose words encourages me a lot.
She is an old lady who has already retired but still teaches math in my school year after year.
I respect her very much and always go back to my high school to visit her.

**5. Are you still in touch with your primary school teachers?**

Yes, I contact with my elementary school teacher on Wechat very often.
The Chinese teacher has give me advice on my career since grade 1.
I always ask her about which option I should choose when I'm hard to decide. And she will ask me several questions and make it clear what will happen when I choose either side.
So we chat a lot and she do me a lot of favor in my teenage.

#### Describe an old friend you had lost touch with and got in contact with again

**Part 2**

I'm going to talk about a friend I hadn't seen for a long time but we met each other a few days before. It's my classmate in primary school called Peter, who is my best friend at that time.

We got to know each other since we both like to play basketball and read books. We always read together and compete in basketball competition as teammates.

After the graduation of elementary school, he went to America to study there, but I still stay in Beijing. Because of the time we were awake are mostly different, we didn't chat.

Just a few days ago, I surprisingly met him somewhere near my primary school. He looks surprised too. We had a lunch together and talked about what we go through in the past few years, and he told me he came back to Beijing to take course in Peking University. It's so amazing that our colleges are near to each other. So we can eat together, read together, play basketball together in the future.

I feel extremely delighted to have my best friend with me again.

**Part 3**

**1. Is it better to have a lot of friends than just a few friends?** *(Giving opinions 观点类)*

It's better to have more friends. we can get more information and ideas from our friends. With more friends we can not only develop our social skills, but also expose ourselves to newest information which we may not get by ourselves.

**2. Would having just a few friends limit your horizons?** *(Giving opinions 观点类)*

Yes, it would limit my horizon.
We can learn a lot of ideas from our friends especially from those who grow up in an environment that is different from our own's.
If we have less friends, we may consider some problems in a narrow perspective rather than a wider on. We can think outside the box if we have more friends.

**3. Do you think you are good at maintaining good relationships with others?** *(Giving opinions 观点类)*

Yes, I believe I'm good at it.
I have not only empathy towards my friends, but also various methods to solve problems and conflicts properly.
For example, when my friends are mad at me, I would first calm them down and then ask them what manner of mine makes them unhappy. After that, I would apologize for my deed.

**4. Why do old friends lose touch with each other?** *(Reasons 原因类)*

There are many factors impacting the relationships.
Firstly, the remote distance prevents friends from contacting each other.
Secondly, we may lose topics as we take jobs or courses that are different from our friends.
Lastly, there will come up with many aspects that are more important than friendship after our maturity, such as our own family and our jobs.

**5. Why do people need to make new friends?** *(Reasons 原因类)*

It's mainly for two reasons.
First, we will lose friends naturally throughout our lives, so it's necessary to build new friendship.
Also, in the different sections of our lives, we should participate in new social networks and expose ourselves to new ideas.

**6. Why do we have to give up some old friends?** *(Reasons 原因类)*

It's a natural process to lessen connection with them.
We will not only struggle to keep in touch with them, but also suffer from the disappointment we get from this relationship, so the best way is to let it go.
